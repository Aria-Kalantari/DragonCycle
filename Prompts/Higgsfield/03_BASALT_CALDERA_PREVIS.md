# Prompt — Basalt Caldera combat previs

Create a 7-second grounded-fantasy gameplay previs shot in a basalt caldera. A massive quadrupedal six-limbed dragon braces in the basin while a lean two-legged wyvern dives from an elevated shelf. The dragon turns its armored shoulder toward the strike, the wyvern aborts at the last moment, banks around a basalt pillar, and wingbeat turbulence throws dust and small debris across the ground.

Third-person gameplay camera 15–25 meters behind and above the dragon, 28–35 mm equivalent lens, readable full silhouettes, restrained camera shake, no cuts, clear spatial continuity, natural late-afternoon light, realistic acceleration and banking, visible weight and displaced air.

Negative constraints: no dodge roll, no teleporting, no instant direction reversal, no hovering, no collision clipping, no extra limbs, no fire breath, no cinematic close-up, no slow motion, no text or UI.
