# Prompt — Live grapple reference

Create a 6-second creature-combat reference clip: a four-legged dragon catches a two-legged wyvern at the wing root with one foreclaw after a low failed pass. Contact begins from believable collision. The dragon plants both hind legs and rotates its torso; the wyvern beats the free wing, twists its tail, and uses momentum to avoid a full pin. Both separate damaged and off-balance.

Side three-quarter camera at gameplay distance, stable 40 mm equivalent lens, full bodies visible, ground contact and weight transfer clear, anticipation-contact-recovery readable, dust only where feet and wing edges strike the ground.

Negative constraints: no perfectly synchronized dance, no canned wrestling pose before contact, no teleporting alignment, no severed limbs, no gore, no extra limbs, no humanoid hands, no weightless bodies, no camera cuts.
